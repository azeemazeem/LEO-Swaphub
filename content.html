The LEO-Swap Hub Solution

A Centralized Digital Depot for co-orbiting commercial stations (CLDs) facilitating Zero-Knowledge Trade to convert stranded assets into vital resources.

Core Innovation: Zero-Knowledge Trade

Zero-Knowledge Digital Ledger

HyperMall ensures complete anonymity by having stations buy from and sell to the Ledger, not directly to each other.

Secure Transactions

Transactions secured via Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) / Orbital Trust Protocol.

Cyber Defense

Displays resources in Risk Bands (Green/Yellow/Red status) instead of precise numbers, preventing competitive spying.

Launch-Cost Credit (LCC) as Currency

Definition

LCC is the digital valuation based on the equivalent cost to launch that mass from Earth.

LCC Value

1 kg = $25,000 LCC

Impact

The universal medium, proving the value of the swap is the value of the launch avoided.

How It Works: The LEO-Swap Process

01

Critical Need Alert

Station Alpha alerts HyperMall for 5kg of Filament. Alpha's "Survival Floor" is nearing depletion.

02

Surplus Offer

Station Beta offers 10kg of surplus Oxygen to HyperMall for LCC credit. Automated Access Tug (AAT) scheduled.

03

Initiate LEO-SWAP

User clicks "INITIATE LEO-SWAP" on dashboard. Anonymous transaction logged on Orbital Ledger.

04

Automated Transfer

AAT receives optimized route (collect Oxygen from Beta, refuel, deliver Filament to Alpha). Physical transfer by robotic arms.
